My favorite part about earning a degree at a music school was that everyone I knew was a fanatic about music. Each of my friends has a brilliant personality with musicality as a major pillar; any passing conversation can easily become a new playlist.

I love discovering new music. I tune through a ton of noise to find songs that I like. If you want some gems without the digging, or if you're like me and just need an artist to kick off some music archeology, feel encouraged to take a moment.
